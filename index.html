Selected Work

Jasper is an AI platform for marketing teams to plan, create, and scale content.

On the Growth team, I focus on activation and adoption of core capabilities. I designed Jasper's first agent-led onboarding experience for Jasper IQ, the system that powers brand knowledge and personalization.

I also designed the first-time experiences for Canvas and Grid—the primary surfaces for content creation—and led the UX strategy for Jasper's credit-based consumption system to help users understand and manage AI usage.

More recently, I've been exploring agent-first product patterns as Jasper evolves toward a more agent-native platform.

PROJECT 01

IQ AGENT

AI agents that power Brand IQ creation, refinement, and governance.

  • Designed an opinionated, agent-led setup flow with human-in-the-loop decisions
  • Agents research company context by synthesizing public information
  • Reduced upfront configuration through lightweight review and refinement
  • Extended beyond setup into IQ editing and ongoing management
  • Introduced governance patterns to support trust, ownership, and scale
PROJECT 02

Canvas
SAMPLE PROJECT

First-time Canvas experience through example-led learning

  • Designed a Sample Project—a pre-built Canvas demonstrating a realistic, multi-channel marketing campaign
  • Paired with an optional guided tour for step-by-step learning
  • Shows how Apps, Chat, project settings, and bulk editing work together
  • Allows users to explore freely or opt into guidance based on their learning style
  • Used coded prototypes to demonstrate interactive tour flows and canvas pan behavior for engineering alignment
PROJECT 03

Grid TEST MODE

Safe experimentation for consumption-based AI credits

  • Designed Test Mode for Grid—a clearly defined, non-billable state for safe experimentation
  • Enabled free generation for designated test rows, visually distinct from billable usage
  • Clearly communicated when actions would consume credits, reducing pricing anxiety
  • Designed the broader consumption-based pricing system across the app, aligning Test Mode with credit logic and usage models
  • Used coded prototypes to make pricing mechanics tangible, helping stakeholders quickly grasp edge cases and tradeoffs
