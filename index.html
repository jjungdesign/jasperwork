THE PROBLEM

As Jasper introduced AI agents to generate brand intelligence, setup became faster—but also riskier. Admins needed clarity, trust, and control without slowing down adoption.

THE SOLUTION

Designing an admin-first setup flow where agents create initial IQs users can immediately evaluate. Establishing lightweight review (accept / reject) with clear AI attribution.

THE IMPACT

Reduced friction in IQ setup while increasing confidence in AI-generated outputs. Created a scalable foundation for future agentic onboarding and governance patterns.
