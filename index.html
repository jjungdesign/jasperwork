PROJECT 01

IQ Agent

Comprehensive analytics platform for tracking campaign performance

Agentic IQ Setup

Agent-driven onboarding for scalable brand intelligence

One-liner

Agents create → Humans evaluate → Systems govern

The problem

As Jasper introduced AI agents to generate brand intelligence, setup became faster—but also riskier.

Admins needed clarity, trust, and control without slowing down adoption.

My role

Product Designer (Growth), partnering closely with PM and Engineering to shape the admin onboarding experience and governance model.

What I focused on

  • Designing an admin-first setup flow where agents create initial IQs users can immediately evaluate
  • Establishing lightweight review (accept / reject) with clear AI attribution
  • Defining lifecycle behaviors beyond first-run, including drop-off, re-entry, and refinement

The design approach

Rather than teaching users what IQ is, the experience drops them into a real workflow.

Automation handles creation; humans stay accountable through simple, intentional decisions.

Outcome

  • Reduced friction in IQ setup while increasing confidence in AI-generated outputs
  • Created a scalable foundation for future agentic onboarding and governance patterns

Scope & collaborators

Onboarding AI systems Governance Lifecycle UX

PM · Engineering · Core IQ partners
